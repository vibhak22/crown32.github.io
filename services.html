DENTURES

Dentures are removable appliances which are used as a replacement of missing teeth and tissues.

DENTAL FILLINGS

Dental Filling is a treatment modality to restore missing tooth structure which could have been a result of decay or trauma.

DENTAL IMPLANTS

Dental implants are easily the most popular and also the ideal solution, for replacing your missing tooth/teeth.

BRACES

For alignment of crooked and irregular teeth using fixed or removable appliances. Ceramic braces, lingual braces and Invisalign are also used for patients with cosmetic needs.

BRIDGES AND CROWNS

Dental Crowns are a tooth-shaped cap which is placed on the tooth to restore the shape, size, and appearance of the tooth.

WISDOM TOOTH REMOVAL

Wisdom teeth often known as third molars are the last teeth to erupt. One usually gets them in late teens or early twenties. However, even instances of eruption in late adulthood are common.

TEETH WHITENING

Teeth whitening is a process of removing stains from the tooth surface and restoring the natural color of the teeth. Whitening is a one-time procedure performed by a dentist.

SMILE MAKEOVER

Smile Makeover is a process of improving the appearance of a smile through various procedures which are cosmetic or surgical.
